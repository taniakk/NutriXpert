🧬 Micronutrients: Vitamins & Minerals Explained

Micronutrients are essential nutrients required in small amounts but play a crucial role in maintaining overall health. They include vitamins and minerals that support immunity, metabolism, and body functions.

A deficiency in micronutrients can lead to fatigue, weak immunity, poor growth, and various health problems. Ensuring a balanced diet helps meet these nutritional needs.

🍊 Vitamins

Vitamins are organic compounds that support different body functions:

  • Vitamin A: Supports vision and immunity
    Deficiency: Night blindness
  • Vitamin B complex: Helps in energy production
    Deficiency: Beriberi, anemia
  • Vitamin C: Boosts immunity and skin health
    Deficiency: Scurvy
  • Vitamin D: Strengthens bones
    Deficiency: Rickets, Osteomalacia
  • Vitamin E: Antioxidant
    Deficiency: Nerve & muscle damage
  • Vitamin K: Blood clotting
    Deficiency: Excessive bleeding

🥗 Sources of Vitamins:

  • Vitamin A: Carrots, spinach, sweet potatoes
  • Vitamin B complex: Whole grains, eggs, meat, legumes
  • Vitamin C: Citrus fruits, amla, tomatoes
  • Vitamin D: Sunlight, milk, fish
  • Vitamin E: Nuts, seeds, vegetable oils
  • Vitamin K: Green leafy vegetables like spinach & broccoli

⛏️ Minerals

Minerals are essential for body structure and function:

  • Calcium: Strong bones
    Deficiency: Weak bones, osteoporosis
  • Iron: Prevents anemia
    Deficiency: Anemia, fatigue
  • Zinc: Immunity
    Deficiency: Weak immunity, slow wound healing
  • Magnesium: Muscle function
    Deficiency: Muscle cramps, weakness
  • Potassium: Heart health
    Deficiency: Muscle weakness, irregular heartbeat

🥗 Sources of Minerals:

  • Calcium: Milk, cheese, yogurt, leafy greens
  • Iron: Spinach, red meat, lentils, jaggery
  • Zinc: Nuts, seeds, whole grains
  • Magnesium: Bananas, nuts, dark chocolate
  • Potassium: Bananas, potatoes, oranges, coconut water

🥗 Sources

Fruits, vegetables, grains, nuts, dairy, and proteins provide essential micronutrients.

⚠️ Deficiency Effects

Can cause fatigue, weak immunity, anemia, and poor bone health.

🦠 Deficiency Diseases

Anemia: Due to Iron deficiency

Scurvy: Vitamin C deficiency

Rickets: Vitamin D deficiency

Night Blindness: Vitamin A deficiency

Micronutrients are essential for overall health and well-being.
