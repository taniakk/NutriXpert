🧬 Micronutrients: Vitamins & Minerals Explained

Micronutrients are essential nutrients required in small amounts but play a crucial role in maintaining overall health. They include vitamins and minerals that support immunity, metabolism, and body functions.

A deficiency in micronutrients can lead to fatigue, weak immunity, poor growth, and various health problems. Ensuring a balanced diet helps meet these nutritional needs.

🍊 Vitamins & Deficiency Diseases

Vitamin A

Vitamin A is essential for good vision, strong immunity, and healthy skin. It helps the eyes to see properly in low light conditions.

Deficiency: Causes poor vision in dim light and weak immune response.

Disease: Night Blindness

Vitamin B Complex

Vitamin B complex helps in energy production, brain function, and red blood cell formation.

Deficiency: Leads to fatigue, weakness, and nerve problems.

Diseases: Beriberi, Anemia

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is important for immunity, skin health, and healing wounds. It also acts as an antioxidant.

Deficiency: Causes weak gums, fatigue, and slow healing.

Disease: Scurvy

Vitamin D

Vitamin D helps the body absorb calcium and keeps bones and teeth strong.

Deficiency: Leads to weak and soft bones.

Diseases: Rickets, Osteomalacia

Vitamin E

Vitamin E acts as an antioxidant and protects cells from damage.

Deficiency: Causes muscle weakness and nerve issues.

Disease: Nerve & Muscle Damage

Vitamin K

Vitamin K is essential for blood clotting and wound healing.

Deficiency: Leads to excessive bleeding and slow clotting.

Disease: Excessive Bleeding

⛏️ Minerals

Minerals are essential for body structure and proper functioning.

Calcium

Calcium helps in building strong bones and teeth. It also supports muscle and nerve function.

Deficiency: Weak bones and increased fracture risk.

Disease: Osteoporosis

Iron

Iron is essential for hemoglobin production and oxygen transport in the body.

Deficiency: Causes fatigue and weakness.

Disease: Anemia

Zinc

Zinc boosts immunity and helps in wound healing and cell growth.

Deficiency: Weak immune system and slow healing.

Disease: Weak Immunity

Magnesium

Magnesium supports muscle and nerve function and helps regulate blood pressure.

Deficiency: Causes muscle cramps and weakness.

Disease: Muscle Cramps

Micronutrients are essential for overall health and well-being.
