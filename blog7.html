🏷️ Reading Food Labels Correctly

Food labels provide important information about what you eat. Understanding how to read them helps you make healthier choices and avoid hidden unhealthy ingredients.

By learning to interpret nutrition labels correctly, you can take control of your diet and improve your overall health.

📏 Check Serving Size

Nutritional values are based on one serving. Eating more increases calorie intake.

🍬 Watch Sugar Content

Avoid hidden sugars like glucose, fructose, and corn syrup.

🧂 Monitor Sodium

High sodium increases blood pressure—choose low-sodium foods.

📋 Read Ingredients

Avoid long lists with artificial additives and preservatives.

⚖️ Nutritional Balance

Choose foods with a balance of protein, carbs, and healthy fats.

🚫 Avoid Misleading Claims

“Low-fat” or “sugar-free” can be misleading—always check labels.

Reading food labels helps you make informed and healthier choices.

👉 Read smart, eat smart, stay healthy!
