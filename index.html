Training & Research

Training Programs

Completed specialized laboratory training programs focusing on modern molecular biology techniques and immunological diagnostic methods.

  • The Basic Recombinant DNA Techniques – DNA Research Centre, Hyderabad. Training covered fundamental molecular biology techniques including DNA isolation, gel electrophoresis, PCR basics, and recombinant DNA applications in research.
  • ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) – R&D Department, Cadila Pharma, Ahmedabad. Hands-on training in ELISA techniques used for antigen–antibody detection, diagnostic testing, and biomedical research applications.

Dissertation Project

Completed a six-month dissertation project titled “Combination System for Removal of Reactive Black 5 from Textile Effluent.” The research focused on developing an effective treatment system for textile wastewater by integrating different removal techniques to reduce dye pollution. The study evaluated treatment efficiency and environmental impact, contributing to sustainable wastewater management approaches in the textile industry.
