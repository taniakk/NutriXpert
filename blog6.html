🔥 Understanding Calories, Protein, Carbs & Fats

To maintain a healthy lifestyle, it’s important to understand how your body gets and uses energy. Calories and macronutrients—protein, carbohydrates, and fats—are the building blocks of your diet.

Each nutrient has a unique role in supporting body functions, growth, and overall well-being.

🔥 What Are Calories?

Calories are units of energy your body uses for daily activities like breathing and movement.

💪 Protein

Protein helps in building and repairing tissues and supports immunity.

🍞 Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the main energy source. Choose whole grains and fruits.

🥑 Fats

Healthy fats support brain and hormone function.

⚖️ Importance of Balance

A balanced diet includes proper proportions of all nutrients.

Understanding macronutrients helps you make better food choices.
