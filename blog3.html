🥗 Myth vs Fact in Nutrition

Nutrition is often filled with myths and misinformation. These misconceptions can lead to unhealthy habits and confusion. Understanding the difference between myths and facts helps you make informed dietary choices.

❌ Myth: Carbs cause weight gain

✅ Fact:

Carbohydrates are essential for energy. Weight gain happens due to excess calorie intake, not carbs alone.

❌ Myth: Skipping meals helps weight loss

✅ Fact:

Skipping meals can slow metabolism and lead to overeating later.

❌ Myth: All fats are bad

✅ Fact:

Healthy fats are essential. Only trans fats should be limited.

❌ Myth: Detox diets are necessary

✅ Fact:

Your body detoxifies naturally through liver and kidneys.

❌ Myth: Protein supplements are always required

✅ Fact:

Most people can meet protein needs through natural foods.

Believing in nutrition myths can harm your health journey. Always rely on evidence-based guidance.

👉 Eat smart, stay informed, and choose facts over myths!
